Privacy Policy for Show First

Last updated: March 2026

Show First ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Show First mobile game. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and protected when you use our application.

By using Show First, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information We Collect

1. Gameplay and Account Data

When you play Show First, the game may automatically generate and store gameplay information necessary for the game to function. This may include:

  • Unique player identifier
  • Game progress and statistics
  • Match results and scores
  • In-game currency balances
  • Gameplay interactions

This information is used solely to operate the game and provide gameplay features.

2. Device Information

The app may collect limited device-related information such as:

  • Device identifiers
  • Device type and operating system
  • App version
  • Performance and crash reports

This information helps maintain stability and improve the app.

3. Advertising Data

Show First may display advertisements provided by third-party advertising networks such as Google AdMob.

These services may collect information such as:

  • Device identifiers
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Interaction with advertisements

This data helps deliver and measure advertising performance.

Learn more about how Google uses data here:

https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Operate and maintain the game
  • Provide multiplayer gameplay functionality
  • Improve game performance and stability
  • Analyze gameplay usage to improve features
  • Display advertisements within the app

Multiplayer Services

Show First includes multiplayer gameplay features. Limited gameplay data may be transmitted to our servers to:

  • Create and manage game rooms
  • Synchronize gameplay between players
  • Maintain match state during gameplay

No personal information such as real names, phone numbers, or email addresses is required to play the game.

Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent user data.

However, limited information may be shared with trusted third-party services strictly for operating the app, including:

  • Advertising services (for example Google AdMob)
  • Analytics and crash reporting tools

These providers process information according to their own privacy policies.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect information transmitted through the app. Data may be transmitted using secure network protocols.

However, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure.

Children's Privacy

Show First is intended for users aged 13 and older.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

Data Retention

Gameplay data may be stored for a limited time to support game functionality, statistics, and system operations. Certain information may be automatically deleted over time.
